Intersting facts about chameleon

  • Almost half of the population of chameleon live in madagascar.
  • Contrary to popular belief, when a chameleon changes its skin color,
    the animal usually isn’t trying to
    camouflage itself by blending into the environment.
    More often, this remarkable ability is used as a way of
    controlling its body temperature.
  • Chameleon's each eye has the incredible
    range of motion of
    90 degrees vertically and 180 degrees
    horizontally.

Intersting facts about pigeon

  • Their navigational skills are so strong that
    they can find their nest from 1300 miles away.
  • In a 2016 study, scientists showed that pigeons can
    differentiate between strings
    of letters and actual words.
  • According to one study,
    they're more efficient multitaskers
